Our Philosophy

We understand that to accomplish your goals, we need to understand your needs completely. We do not immediately jump to a solution we imagine, but instead explore possibilities thoroughly so that the right problems are solved from your point of view. Our designs are thoughtful and functional and as they unfold, each challenge is addressed at the proper time.

We welcome collaborative processes and we know how to generate consensus. We can form and lead consultant teams, supporting the design and construction process so that you meet your schedule and budget.

Woodcock Design has worked with institutions, government agencies, non-profits, developers, homeowners, and businesses to help them reach their goals. We love every aspect of the design process from early concepts through permit approvals, from fundraising to neighborhood meetings, from groundbreaking through final occupancy.

Our skill set will support you every step of the way.
